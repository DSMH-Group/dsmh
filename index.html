Translation Services

DSMH specializes in translating Japanese comics, including manga and doujinshi, to make them accessible to a global audience. We work closely with both the original authors and individuals who require translations for personal use. Our services include:

  • Translation

    Manga Translation

    Manga/Doujinshi Lettering

    Manga/Doujinshi Cleaning

    Manga/Doujinshi Redrawing

    Proofreading services

Unlock Global Readership for your Manga!

Are you a talented Japanese Mangaka with a captivating story waiting to be discovered by a worldwide audience? At DSMH, we understand the passion and dedication that go into creating manga masterpieces. We offer a unique opportunity for mangaka like you to share your work with fans across the globe.

  • Professional Translation:
  • Our expert translators ensure your story’s nuances and emotion is preserved as your works are translated for different audiences.
  • Typesetting and Redrawing:
  • We go beyond translation. If you wish, our team can expertly typeset and redraw your manga to suit different markets, while maintaining your artistic vision.
  • Beyond Manga:
  • While we specialize in manga, we’re open to translating other forms of media like visual novels, light novels, video games, and more!
